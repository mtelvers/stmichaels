FOR OUR PRAYERS:

We pray for the advancement of unity between churches, and give thanks for signs of a renewal in dialogue between
the Anglican and Roman Catholic churches; for greater appreciation of the value and significance of the Sacraments,
and especially the Eucharist, within the Church of England, and for renewal generally across the Church of England;
for a deepening of faith and practice in our attendance at Mass at St Michaels; for those who receive the Blessed
Sacrament from St Michaels at home or in care homes, that they may feel within themselves the reality of being part
of the Body of Christ, and be sustained by the grace and blessing of the Eucharist in their lives.
