PARISH PILGRIMAGE TO WALSINGHAM 26th – 29th May, coinciding with School Holidays.

                 A list for you to book your places on this Parish Pilgrimage is at Back of Church. Please add your
                 name if you wish to go.

                 Walsingham, a shrine to the Virgin Mary set in a quiet Norfolk village, has been a place of
                 pilgrimage since 1061. Pilgrimage to Walsingham is a unique and joyful mix of inspiring worship of
                 the highest quality, opportunities for quiet reflection and healing ministry, and fun and laughter with
                 fellow pilgrims from parishes across the UK set amidst beautiful countryside.

                 It is a place richly blessed by God, and a good way of deepening friendships with others from St
                 Michael’s congregation.

If you want to know more about Walsingham, please see the information at the back of church or ask Fr John.
