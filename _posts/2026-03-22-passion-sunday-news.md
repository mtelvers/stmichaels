This Week: Mass on Thursday at 10.30. On Wednesday the Church celebrates the feast of The Annunciation.

EASTER LILIES IN MEMORY OF A LOVED ONE

If you would like to sponsor a Lily in church this Easter in memory of a departed loved one, please add your name to
the list at the Back of Church as soon as possible and let Margaret Gatehouse (or a Churchwarden) have your £5.

HOLY WEEK: Next Sunday is Palm Sunday: Procession and Sung Mass at 10.30am. The Chrism Mass is at noon in
Rochester Cathedral on Tuesday 31st March. This is an opportunity to gather together with our friends, both clergy and
laity, from all the Society Parishes across Rochester and Canterbury dioceses, to unite together in our faithful witness
as Catholic Christians in Holy Week, and to show our support for our Bishop, +Luke. Do please attend if you can.

Maundy Thursday: There are lists at Back of Church for you to sign up for the Washing of Feet at the Mass of the
Last Supper at 7.30pm, and for The Watch until Midnight.
